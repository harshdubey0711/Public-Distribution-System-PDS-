About the Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PM-KMY) is a central sector scheme for Small and Marginal Farmers (SMFs), offering a fixed monthly pension of ₹3,000 after the age of 60. The scheme ensures old age security and social welfare.

Benefits

  • Monthly pension of ₹3,000 after age 60.
  • Family pension available to spouse in case of subscriber's death.
  • In case of subscriber’s death before vesting, spouse may continue or withdraw contributions.

Eligibility

  • Age: 18–40 years
  • Landholding: Up to 2 hectares of cultivable land
  • Not a member of EPFO, NPS, or ESIC
  • Not an income taxpayer

Enrollment Process

Eligible farmers can visit their nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) to enroll. They need to make a monthly contribution (₹55–₹200 depending on age) till the age of 60. The government contributes an equal amount.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Landholding records
  • Savings bank account details
  • Mobile number
