Details

Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PM-GKAY) is a scheme under Atmanirbhar Bharat to supply free food grains to migrants and the poor.

  • Phase I & II: April–June 2020 and July–November 2020
  • Phase III: May–June 2021
  • Phase IV: July–November 2021
  • Phase V: December 2021–March 2022
  • Phase VI: April–September 2022 (Subsidy: ₹80,000 Crore)

The scheme provides 5kg of free food grains monthly, in addition to subsidized ration under the NFSA.

Note: Check the One Nation One Ration Card scheme for portability of benefits across India.

Benefits

  • 5 kg of free food grain per person per month under PMGKAY
  • 5 kg of subsidized food grain under PDS

State-wise Distribution:

  • Wheat: Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, Chandigarh, Delhi, Gujarat
  • Rice: All other States/UTs

Eligibility

  • Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) families
  • Priority Households (PHH) identified by State/UT authorities
  • Households led by widows, disabled, terminally ill, or elderly persons without support
  • Single men/women with no family or support
  • Primitive tribal groups, daily wage earners, and informal workers
  • HIV-positive persons from BPL families

Application Process (Offline)

  1. Visit the nearest Fair Price Shop with your Ration Card
  2. Quote either Ration Card number or Aadhaar number
  3. Authenticate identity via fingerprint or iris scan

Documents Required

  • Ration Card
  • Aadhaar Card (if linked to Ration Card)
